Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 30, 2025

This website values your privacy. The following outlines how data is collected, used, and protected when visiting rogerehmpke.com.

What Data Is Collected

Contact Form: If you submit a message via the contact form, your name, email address, and message content are collected solely to respond to your inquiry. No data is sold or reused for marketing.

Analytics: Google Analytics is used to assess site performance and traffic. It may collect data such as browser type, location (city-level), and pages visited. This data is anonymized whenever possible and used strictly to improve the site experience.

AI Assistant (GPT Chat):

  • Your question is submitted to a secure AWS Lambda function when using the chat interface.
  • The question is securely transmitted to OpenAI’s GPT model via encrypted API.
  • Responses are generated based on predefined site context and your input.
  • No personal data is stored on our server; however, your browser may temporarily retain chat history using sessionStorage for continuity.
  • Please avoid including sensitive personal data in chat interactions.

How Data Is Used

Your contact form submission is used solely to respond to your message. It is not shared or sold.

Analytics data is used to improve the site and understand what content is helpful — not to identify or track individuals.

Third-Party Services

Google Analytics may collect and process data according to Google's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

If you'd like to request deletion of your message or have questions about this policy, please reach out directly via the contact form.
