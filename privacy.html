Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 30, 2025 • Last updated: October 26, 2025

This website values your privacy. This policy explains how data is collected, used, and protected when visiting rogerehmpke.com.

What Data We Collect

Contact Form: If you submit a message via the contact form, your name, email address, and message are collected solely to respond to your inquiry. No data is sold, shared, or reused for marketing purposes.

Analytics: Google Analytics 4 (via Google Tag Manager) is used to understand site performance and user interaction. It may collect anonymized technical data such as browser type, device, screen size, approximate location (city-level), and pages visited. IP addresses are not stored, and reports are aggregated. Analytics data helps improve site structure, content, accessibility, and performance.

Server and Security Logs: Our hosting provider automatically collects limited technical information—such as IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and access timestamps—for the purpose of maintaining security, detecting abuse, and ensuring system reliability. These logs are not used to personally identify visitors.

Site Chat Assistant:

  • Your question is transmitted securely via HTTPS to an AWS Lambda function.
  • The message is then processed by OpenAI's GPT API to generate a contextual response.
  • No personal data is stored or logged on our servers.
  • Your browser may temporarily store chat history in sessionStorage for your convenience.
  • Please avoid submitting sensitive or personal information in chat interactions.

How Data Is Used

  • To respond to inquiries submitted through the contact form.
  • To improve the usability, content, and accessibility of the website.
  • To assess site performance using anonymized analytics data.
  • To process chat input in real time to provide context-based responses (without data retention).

Third-Party Services

The following services support this site's functionality and security:

  • Google Tag Manager/Analytics: Collects anonymized usage data and event metrics.
  • OpenAI: Provides the GPT model that powers the site's chat assistant.
  • AWS (Amazon Web Services): Hosts the backend service that securely delivers chat requests and responses.

Each third party adheres to its own privacy policy and complies with applicable data protection standards.
You can review their policies here:

  • Google: policies.google.com/privacy
  • OpenAI: openai.com/privacy
  • Amazon Web Services: aws.amazon.com/privacy

Your Rights & Contact

You may request deletion of messages submitted via the contact form or ask questions about this policy by using the contact form.

This privacy policy will be updated as needed to reflect new tools, AI functionality, or legal compliance requirements.
