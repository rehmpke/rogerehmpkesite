About Roger

I’m a digital strategist with 25+ years of experience leading web systems, accessibility, and governance in higher ed and mission-driven environments.

At Kankakee Community College, I function as Director of Digital Strategy — guiding CMS architecture, cross-departmental governance, and compliance execution for both public and internal digital platforms. I collaborate with teams across Marketing, ITS, Foundation, and Student Services to ensure our systems are accessible, aligned with policy, and built for long-term sustainability.

I also work with small teams and nonprofit leaders to bring clarity to complex web platforms—reducing risk, avoiding tool sprawl, and building systems that serve people, not just technology.

Whether advising on accessibility, content strategy, or institutional compliance touchpoints, my work is built around one principle: clarity leads to trust.
